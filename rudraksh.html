Key Historical Figures

Ashoka the Great

304-232 BCE

Third emperor of the Mauryan Empire, known for spreading Buddhism and his principles of dharma.

Mahatma Gandhi

1869-1948 CE

Leader of India's independence movement, known for his philosophy of non-violence.

Bhagat Singh

1907-1931 CE

A revolutionary freedom fighter who inspired youth across India with his sacrifice and patriotism.

Subhas Chandra Bose

1897-1945 CE

A prominent nationalist leader who founded the Indian National Army (INA) during World War II.

Jawaharlal Nehru

1889–1964 CE

First Prime Minister of India, a central figure in Indian politics before and after independence.
